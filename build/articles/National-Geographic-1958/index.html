National Geographic CXIV - Volumes 2-4, 1958

—October 24

I couldn't resist these when I saw them on the Etsy store of Peony & Thistle. They're full of glorious mid-century design, the covers alone are wonderful, and the advertising inside is like going for a wander around the set of Mad Men.

I've found myself leaning on Magazine's more and more for inspiration of late. Monocle was a great source of ideas when I was reworking this website, and Off Screen manages to inspire me to be a better design whilst letting me read about designers. These old editions of National Geographic will join those other titles in providing something pretty and well designed to enjoy in those few hours a day when I'm not staring at a screen.
